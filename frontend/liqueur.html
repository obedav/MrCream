Signature Recipe: Turbo Espresso Martini

Ingredients:
  • 60ml Turbo Cream Liqueur
  • 30ml Vodka
  • 30ml Fresh Espresso
  • Coffee beans for garnish
  • Ice
Method:
  1. Add all ingredients to cocktail shaker with ice
  2. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds
  3. Double strain into chilled martini glass
  4. Garnish with 3 coffee beans
